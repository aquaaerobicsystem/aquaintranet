🗺️ Workflow Overview

Every capital expenditure request follows a structured approval pipeline. Here's the complete flow from start to finish:

Who Can Do What

📝 All Employees

Visit aqua-aerobic.net:5030 to create a new expenditure request. No login required.

📧 Managers & President

Approve or deny requests exclusively via the email link sent when a request reaches your approval stage.

🔐 Accounting

Full access to Dashboard, Financial, Admin, and the ability to remove and restore requests. Click "🔒 Login" in the nav bar or navigate to a restricted page to log in.

🔧 IT Department

Full app access (same as Accounting) plus SMTP mail server configuration. Uses a separate IT Passcode via the same Login button.

1
📝 Requestor Submits
Any employee visits aqua-aerobic.net:5030 and fills out the procurement form with vendor, cost, description, and their digital signature.
2
🏢 Department Review
The department manager receives an email with a direct link and approves or denies the request with their signature.
3
💰 Financial Review (Accounting)
Accounting logs in with their UserID & passcode, verifies fund availability, enters accounting details, and approves.
4
👑 Presidential Review
Only required when the estimated cost exceeds $25,000. The president receives an email link for final sign-off.
Conditional — Over $25K Only
✓
✅ Approved!
Request is fully approved. A printable form with all signatures is available.

📝 How to Submit a Request

1

Visit the App

Open your browser and go to http://www.aqua-aerobic.net:5030/ — no login is required. The Home page shows a summary of all request counts and a prominent "Create New Request" button. Click it to open the submission form.

2

Fill Out the Form

Complete all required fields (marked with *):

  • Vendor — The supplier or vendor name
  • Estimated Start Date — When you expect the work to begin
  • Description — Detailed description of the expenditure
  • Estimated Cost — Total estimated cost in USD
  • Your Full Name — Your legal name for the signature
  • Your Email — Enter your Aqua user ID (the system appends @aqua-aerobic.com)
  • Department Manager — Name and email of your department manager
💡 If the estimated cost exceeds $25,000, the request will automatically require Presidential approval after Financial approval.
3

Attach Supporting Documents

Upload any relevant quotes, invoices, or supporting documents. Accepted formats: PDF, Word, Excel. Maximum 10MB per file. You can drag and drop files or click to browse.

4

Generate Your Signature

Enter your full name in the "Requested By" field, then click "✍️ Generate Signature". A digital signature will be created from your name. This signature is mandatory for submission.

⚠️ You cannot submit a request without a signature. Make sure to enter your full name first, then generate the signature.
5

Submit

Click "Submit Request". Two emails are automatically sent:

  • An approval request email to your department manager
  • A confirmation email to you with a link to view/edit your request

After submission you are taken back to the Home page. The status will show as ⏳ Pending Dept.

6

View, Edit & Track Your Request

Your confirmation email includes a "View / Edit Request" button that links directly to your request. Use this link to:

  • View your request details and current approval status at any time
  • Edit your request while it is still in "Pending Department" status (click the ✏️ Edit Request button)
🔖 Bookmark the link from your confirmation email! You'll also receive status update emails with the same link whenever your request is approved or denied at each stage.
⚠️ Once your department manager has reviewed the request, editing is no longer available. If you edit a request, a notification email is automatically sent to your department manager about the changes.

🏢 Department Approval

For Department Managers
1

Open the Email Notification

When a request is submitted, you will receive an email notification with the request details and a direct link to review it.

📧 The email contains a unique link for each request (e.g. #department?id=123). This is the only way to access a specific request for review.
2

Click the Link to Review

Click the direct link in the email. This will open the app and take you straight to the request details for that specific Request ID — including vendor, cost, description, requestor info, attached documents, and the requestor's signature.

⚠️ You must use the link from the email to review a request. The link contains the Request ID and will load the correct request automatically.
3

Approve or Deny

✓ To Approve

Click the green "✓ Approve Request" button. Enter your name, generate your digital signature, confirm the date, and add any optional comments. Then click "Confirm".

✗ To Deny

Click the red "✗ Deny Request" button. Enter your name and provide a mandatory reason for denial. This action cannot be undone.

💰 Financial Approval

Accounting Department Only
🔐 This section applies to the Accounting department and IT staff. Both have full access to the app using their respective passcodes.
1

Log In & Open the Financial Page

Click "🔒 Login" in the navigation bar (or click "Financial" in the nav to be prompted automatically). Enter your Aqua UserID and passcode. Requests that have passed department approval will appear here.

2

Enter Accounting Details

When you review a request, an Accounting Details form will appear. Fill in the relevant fields:

  • B Number — Internal budget number
  • Fixed Asset Account — GL account for fixed assets
  • Property Type — Classification of the asset
  • Fiscal Year — e.g., FY2026
  • Entered By — Your name
  • Class — Asset class
  • Accum. Depreciation Account — Accumulated depreciation GL account
  • Depreciation Expense Account — Expense GL account
  • UseTax / Equipment Exempt / Tooling — Toggle checkboxes as applicable

Click "💾 Save Accounting Details" to save before approving.

3

Approve or Deny

Same process as Department approval — enter your name, generate your signature, and confirm. Upon approval:

  • If cost ≤ $25,000 → Request is fully approved
  • If cost > $25,000 → Request moves to Presidential Approval

👑 Presidential Approval

For Executive/CEO
1

When Is This Required?

Presidential approval is only required for requests with an estimated cost exceeding $25,000. After financial approval, an email notification is sent to the presidential/CEO email address.

2

Open the Email Notification

You will receive an email notification containing the request details and a direct link to review it.

📧 The email contains a unique link for the request (e.g. #presidential?id=456). This is the only way to access a specific request for presidential review.
3

Click the Link & Review

Click the direct link in the email. The app will open directly to the request, showing all details including prior department and financial approvals. Then approve or deny using the same signature workflow as other approval stages.

⚠️ You must use the link from the email to review a request. The link contains the Request ID and will load the correct request automatically.

🖨️ Printing a Request Form

Accounting Department Only
🔐 Printing is restricted to Accounting and IT staff. Log in via the "🔒 Login" button in the nav bar to access the Dashboard where the Print button is available.
1

When Can I Print?

The "🖨 Print" button becomes available once a request has been fully approved. You'll find it in the Actions column on the Dashboard.

2

Print the Form

Click "🖨 Print" to open a print preview modal with a formatted report including all request details, signatures, and accounting information. Click "🖨️ Print" in the modal footer to send to your printer or save as PDF.

⚙️ Admin Settings

Accounting & IT
🔐 The Admin page is restricted to Accounting and IT staff. Click "🔒 Login" in the nav bar or navigate to the Admin page to be prompted. Use your Aqua UserID and App Passcode (Accounting) or IT Passcode (IT).
1

Activity Logs

The Admin page shows a log of all system actions — submissions, approvals, denials, and removals with timestamps. Use the "↻ Refresh" button to update the log.

2

Removed Requests

Click the "🗑️ Removed Requests" header to expand the section. This shows all soft-deleted requests with their original status. Click "♻ Restore" to bring a request back to its previous status.

3

Email & System Settings

Click the "Email & System Settings" header to expand the settings panel. Here you can configure:

  • Accounting Email — Where financial approval notifications are sent
  • Presidential Email — Where CEO approval notifications are sent (for requests > $25K)
  • App Passcode — The authentication code used for Accounting login and removing requests
4

Removing & Restoring Requests

To remove a request, click the "🗑 Remove" button in the Actions column. You'll need to provide:

  • The requestor's full name (must match the original requestor)
  • The system passcode
💡 Removed requests are not permanently deleted. They can be restored from the Admin page → Removed Requests section. Click "♻ Restore" to bring a request back to its previous status.
5

SMTP Configuration IT Only

SMTP mail server settings are managed in a separate section on the Admin page, restricted to IT personnel. To access:

  • Expand the "🔧 SMTP Configuration" panel
  • Enter the IT Passcode to unlock the settings (default: IT2026)
  • Configure the SMTP host, port, credentials, TLS, and From address
  • Click "💾 Save SMTP Settings" to apply changes
  • Use the "🔑 Change IT Passcode" section at the bottom to update the code
💡 The IT Passcode also works as a full-access login for the entire app (Dashboard, Financial, Admin). IT staff can use their IT passcode in the standard login prompt to access all areas.
⚠️ The IT Passcode is completely separate from the App Passcode and is not visible to Accounting staff. Only IT can view and change it from within the unlocked SMTP section. Change it from the default immediately after first use.
