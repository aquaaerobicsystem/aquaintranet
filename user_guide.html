Feature 1

🔍Quick Search

The Search bar sits at the top of the Employee Hub. Type any keyword and press Enter to find files, folders, or tools instantly.

What it looks like
🔍 Search the Employee Hub…

How to use it

1
Click the search bar at the top of the page It's labeled "Search the Employee Hub" and is always visible.
2
Type your keyword Enter a word, part number, department name, or topic (e.g. "Concur", "HR Forms", "174523").
3
Press Enter or click Search Results appear or AquaSearch launches to find matching documents.
💡 If Quick Search doesn't find what you need, try AquaSearch — it searches inside documents, not just file names. Jump to AquaSearch ↓
Feature 2

⚡Quick Access Chips

Quick Access chips appear just below the header as a scrolling row of buttons. Each chip is a one-click shortcut to a frequently used tool or resource — no searching required.

Example chips
📊 Dashboard ✈️ Concur Travel 💰 Paycom 🗓️ Outlook Calendar 📦 Product Catalog
ℹ️ Chips can be scrolled left and right on smaller screens. If you don't see the one you need, scroll right or check the Directory below.
Feature 3

📁Directory

The Directory is the main body of the Employee Hub. It's organized into department categories (e.g., HR, Engineering, Operations, Sales) with collapsible sub-folders inside each one.

Navigating the Directory

  • Click a category name to expand or collapse it.
  • Sub-folders inside a category can also be expanded by clicking.
  • Click any link inside a folder to open the file or resource directly.
  • If a folder is missing from your department, contact IT — it may need to be added.
Feature 4

🏢Company Resources

At the bottom of the Employee Hub you'll find a Company Resources section — a dedicated area for company-wide documents that don't belong to a single department:

  • Employee manuals and handbooks
  • Corporate policies and procedures
  • Benefits and compliance documents
  • Frequently referenced forms
ℹ️ Company Resources links appear as chips at the bottom of the page, similar to Quick Access chips at the top.
Feature 5 — Can't find it?

🔎AquaSearch

Can't find what you're looking for? AquaSearch is your power tool. Unlike a normal search that only looks at file names, AquaSearch reads the actual text inside documents — PDFs, Word files, and Excel spreadsheets.

💡 Example: You remember a phrase like "2026 Rental Business Initiatives" was in a document but have no idea where it's saved. Type it into AquaSearch and it will find the file instantly.

When to use AquaSearch

  • You remember words from inside a document but not the filename.
  • You know a part number or project code (e.g., 174523).
  • You searched Quick Search and didn't find what you need.
  • You want to find all documents related to a topic (e.g., all Benefits documents).

How to launch AquaSearch

1
Type your search term in the Quick Search bar Use keywords you'd expect to find inside the document — a phrase, name, part number, etc.
2
Press Enter AquaSearch will launch and begin scanning documents for your term.
3
Review your results AquaSearch shows matching files with highlighted snippets of the text it found. Click any result to open the document.

Submitting Test Results

1
Click "Submit Testing Results" The section expands with a form.
2
Enter your full name and the access code The access code was provided with this email — ask IT if you don't have it.
3
Check off items you tested and add any notes File Links, Search, Layout & Structure. Add any general comments in the Notes box.
4
Click "Submit Testing Results" Your entry is logged immediately and visible in the table below.

Reporting an Issue

1
Click "Report an Issue" The form expands below the Testing Results form.
2
Enter your name, access code, and select a category Categories: Broken Link, Search Issue, Missing File/Folder, or Other.
3
Describe the issue and optionally attach files You can drag & drop or click to attach screenshots, PDFs, or Word documents.
4
Click "Submit Issue" IT reviews submissions in real time.
📌 You can also edit your own submission after the fact by clicking the ✏️ Edit button next to your entry and re-entering your name and access code. Need to delete something? Contact ckonkol@aqua-aerobic.com or cshields@aqua-aerobic.com.
For Department Leads

⚙️Managing the Hub

The Employee Hub is managed through a visual Admin Portal — no coding required. IT currently handles all updates, but anyone can be trained to manage their own department's section.

  • Add, edit, or remove links and folders through a point-and-click interface
  • Upload custom icons for links
  • Reorder categories and links with up/down buttons
  • Publish changes instantly — no waiting for IT
ℹ️ If your department would like to manage its own section of the Hub, reach out to IT at ckonkol@aqua-aerobic.com to request training and access.
