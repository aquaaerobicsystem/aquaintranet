User Guide

How to use your new Employee Hub

Welcome to the Employee Hub

The newly redesigned Aqua-Aerobic Employee Hub is your central location for finding company tools, dashboards, HR resources, and manuals. This guide will help you navigate the new layout efficiently.

1. Quick Search

At the top right of the homepage, you'll find the main search bar. Typing a query and pressing Enter allows you to quickly locate files and resources across the intranet.

2. Quick Access

Directly below the header, you will see a scrolling list of Quick Access chips. These are one-click shortcuts to the most frequently used tools in the company, like the Company Dashboard or Concur Travel.

3. Directory

The main body of the Hub is divided into department categories (e.g., HR, Engineering, Operations). Clicking on a category will expand it to show related folders, links, and documents specific to that area.

4. Company Resource

You'll also find a dedicated section for general Company Resources, providing quick access to employee manuals, corporate policies, and other essential company-wide documents.

5. AquaSearch

If you cannot find what you are looking for using the above methods, you can use AquaSearch! AquaSearch doesn't just look at file names—it reads the actual text inside PDFs, Word documents, and Excel spreadsheets. If you remember a phrase inside a document but forgot where it's saved, AquaSearch will find it for you.

Managing the Hub: Currently, the IT department has access to the Admin Portal to make layout changes and add new links. However, the system is designed to be completely user-friendly via a visual GUI Builder! Anyone can be trained to make changes. If your department wants to manage its own section of the hub, please contact IT to request training and access.
